Q & Ace is a smart digital assessment book for upper primary students following the MOE syllabus in Singapore.

Why Q & Ace?

Real Time Feedback

Q & Ace’s active feedback mechanism ensures that you will be able to track and monitor which skills are in greater need of attention, and be able to prepare for exams efficiently.

Click for free trial

Recorded Video Explanations

No more worrying over slots and schedules. Our worksheets come with pre-recorded video explanations, so you can watch them at your own convenience, as many times as you need.


All questions are carefully recorded and verified by MOE educators and experienced tutors.

Click for free trial

Work Smarter, Not Harder.

All questions are tailored based on the individual student’s grasp of the subject. Our algorithm determines the student’s current proficiency level as they solve questions. This way, students are challenged according to their level of understanding without feeling discouraged.

It allows every student the opportunity to improve at their own speed. As parents, you will also receive customized feedback of your child’s strengths and weaknesses to better prepare for exams.
