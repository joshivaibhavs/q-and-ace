The Power of
AI-learning.

Q & Ace is a smart digital assessment book for upper primary students following the MOE syllabus in Singapore.

Developed by data specialists, and carefully vetted by MOE educators as well as experienced tutors, this platform offers customised learning that is tailored to improve your child’s understanding and help them ace their exams.

Work Smarter, Not Harder

All attempted questions are tracked so that you and your child will always know how they are performing and which areas they need to work on. Our algorithm determines the student’s current proficiency level as they solve questions and tailors subsequent questions that challenge them without feeling discouraged.

It allows every student the opportunity to improve at their own speed. As parents, you will also receive customized feedback of your child’s strengths and weaknesses to better prepare for exams.
