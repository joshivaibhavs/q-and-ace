About

Work Smarter

At Q & Ace, we want our students to enjoy the process of learning. We believe that each child learns and improves at their own pace. Our algorithm determines the student’s proficiency level as they solve questions and tailors subsequent questions to challenge them without making them feel discouraged. Every question comes with clearly written explanations and questions worth 3 marks and above even have video explanations from our experienced teachers.

Parents will also receive customised feedback of their child’s strengths and weaknesses to better prepare for exams without having to flip through stacks of assessment books and past year papers. All attempted questions are tracked so that you and your child will always know how they are performing and which areas need more work. Use online learning to work smarter with Q & Ace!

Preetha’s strong passion for teaching has led her to accumulate 14 years of tutoring experience. As an MOE-trained teacher who has taught all levels from Primary 1 to 6 across her six years of teaching in primary school, she understands the areas students typically find challenging through their formative years.
